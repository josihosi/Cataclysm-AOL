# Bandit repeated site activity reinforcement seam v0 (2026-04-20)

Status: active greenlit contract.

## Promotion audit verdict

The smoke bridge is checkpointed.
The light bridge is checkpointed.
The human / route bridge is checkpointed.
The next narrow visibility promotion is the repeated site-centered reinforcement bridge.

Current bandit seams already know how to carry repeated smoke, light, and traffic marks on the ledger.
What they still do not have is a bounded reinforcement rule that turns mixed repeated site-centered activity into stronger revisit interest without inventing a separate magical settlement-signature class.

This contract is the next honest bite after human / route.
It keeps the visibility packet moving without jumping into broad concealment law, city-signature mythology, or a full persistence-heavy traffic history simulator.

## Scope

1. Build a bounded reinforcement adapter that turns repeated site-centered smoke, light, and human / route activity into modest confidence and bounty amplification on existing site-shaped marks.
2. Feed that reinforcement through the current mark-generation / playback seam so repeated mixed evidence becomes reviewer-readable mark strengthening instead of hand-authored scenario lore.
3. Preserve the product rule that repeated ordinary signals should usually reinforce revisit value first, not become free identity truth, free loot truth, or an automatic raid warrant.
4. Preserve the anti-magic rule that this remains an aggregation of ordinary signals, not a new global settlement-signature family.
5. Expose the reinforcement packet and resulting mark/lead path in reviewer-readable report output so later scenario and 500-turn proof work can reuse the seam.

## Non-goals

- broad player/basecamp concealment implementation
- smoke, light, or human / route adapter rework
- city-activity special casing
- sound or horde-attraction expansion
- full traffic-history persistence
- the first full 500-turn proof itself

## Success shape

This item is good enough when:
1. a bounded repeated-site reinforcement adapter exists from mixed repeated smoke/light/traffic footing into modest site-mark confidence and bounty amplification
2. deterministic coverage proves the key bounded distinctions: mixed repeated signals reinforce one site mark cleanly, weak repetition does not fake durable settlement truth, self-corroboration stays bounded, and strengthened site interest still does not unlock free extraction jobs
3. the reinforced marks feed the current bandit mark-generation, playback, and evaluator seams reviewer-cleanly
4. the slice stays visibly bounded and does not smuggle in a broader concealment system, a new settlement-signature family, or the 500-turn proof packet

## Validation expectations

- prefer narrow deterministic bandit coverage and small repeated-site scenario extensions on the current playback footing
- the first honest code validation should stay `make -j4 tests` and `./tests/cata_test "[bandit]"`
- keep evidence reviewer-readable; do not hide the reinforcement seam inside debugger soup or broad live-harness theater unless deterministic proof stops being enough
